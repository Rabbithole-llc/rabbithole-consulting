Why start here

Don't deploy AI before you know where it actually wins.

Most businesses waste six months and a lot of money chasing the wrong AI projects. They start with the shiny use case, not the high-leverage one. By the time they figure out which workflows actually compound, they've already burned the budget.

The AI Operations Audit fixes that. Before we ever build anything, we map your business through an AI workforce lens, identify the 3–5 highest-leverage opportunities, and tell you exactly what to build, in what order, with what expected gain.

What you get

Four deliverables. Two weeks. One clear plan.

1. Workflow map

A visual map of how work actually moves through your business — where time goes, where money leaks, where bottlenecks live.

2. Leverage analysis

The 3–5 places in your operation where deploying an AI operator would create the biggest gain in revenue, throughput, or cost.

3. Deployment roadmap

A prioritized build order with rough timelines, tools, and integrations. The plan you'd hand to any team to execute.

4. Strategy session

A live working session where we walk you through every finding, answer questions, and stress-test the plan with you.

How it works

Two weeks from kickoff to roadmap.

Week 1 — Discovery. Two working sessions with you and (optionally) your team. We walk through every revenue-producing and operational workflow, ask hard questions, and pull data from the tools you already use.

Week 2 — Synthesis & delivery. We build the workflow map, analyze the leverage points, and assemble the roadmap. Final session to walk you through it, answer questions, and adjust priorities together.

Pricing is a flat upfront engagement. Sized on the strategy call based on the size and complexity of your operation.

FAQ

Common questions before you book.

What does an AI Operations Audit include?

A structured walkthrough of your business operations, a workflow map showing where AI can add the most leverage, a prioritized list of AI operator opportunities, and a deployment roadmap with rough timelines and cost estimates. You leave the audit with a plan you can act on whether you hire us to build it or not.

How long does the audit take?

Most audits run one to two weeks from kickoff. We need 2–3 working sessions with you and access to a few of your operational tools.

Do I have to hire you to build what the audit recommends?

No. The audit deliverable is yours. Many clients use it as a roadmap they execute internally. Many others ask us to build it for them. Both are fine.

How much does an AI Operations Audit cost?

The audit is sold as a flat upfront engagement. Exact pricing depends on the size of your operation. We size it on the strategy call.

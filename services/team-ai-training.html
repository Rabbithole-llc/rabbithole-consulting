Format options

Three ways to run it.

Half-day intensive. Up to 12 people. Best for small teams or a leadership group that needs to get fluent fast. Three to four hours, hands-on the whole way.

Full-day deep dive. Up to 20 people. Morning is foundations and live demos on your workflows; afternoon is building and shipping real prompt libraries together.

Multi-week program. For larger teams or businesses doing a real AI rollout. Four to six weekly sessions with assignments in between, ending with a working AI playbook for the company.

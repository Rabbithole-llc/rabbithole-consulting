Voice
How Magdalena 1163 sounds in any channel
Strong

Considered, modern-romantic, design-forward. Headlines lean serif and declarative: "Crafted for the Curious," "Your Morning, Unhurried," "Evenings are better up here." Body copy is short, sensory, and quietly confident — we describe the bath tray, the curtains, the hour before the rooftop fills. We write to a guest who already books well; we don't oversell. Never "luxury," never "experience" as a noun, never call ourselves anything but Magdalena 1163.

tagline: "Crafted for the Curious" serif-leaning headlines sensory specifics Condado · San Juan · PR
Audience
Who we're writing for
Strong

Two distinct segments. Leisure: design-leaning couples 28–55, US East Coast (NY/NJ/DC), secondary markets Miami + LA, plus PR diaspora returning home for weekends and milestones. They book direct when given a reason. Business + bleisure: mid-market execs flying into Condado for board meetings, conferences, and short stays — the conference center, fast WiFi, and room service let us play above our 10-suite size. Both segments value design over scale.

Do say
Phrases and concepts the brand owns
  • · "Crafted for the Curious" (tagline)
  • · "Stay where curiosity lives"
  • · "the heart of Condado"
  • · Rooftop Lounge · Byredo amenities · complimentary breakfast
  • · "Business class. Boutique soul."
  • · Studio · Junior Suite · Two-Bedroom Suite (canonical room names)
Don't say
Things off-brand or off-limits
  • · "Casa Magdalena" (canonical brand is Magdalena 1163 — strict)
  • · "luxury," "experience" (as noun), "elevate"
  • · "paradise," "hidden gem," "tropical escape"
  • · any reference to all-inclusive resorts
  • · competitor names (Aman, O:live, Acacia, Canario)
  • · superlatives ("best," "most beautiful")
Visual style
Auto-injected into every image generation
Strong

Warm interior light, walnut + cream + olive palette, gold details. Every room shot is composed — the bath tray placed deliberately, the linen smoothed, the matches arranged in a wood box. Architectural lines respected. Rooftop and exterior shots taken at golden hour. Serif type overlay (Magdalena 1163 wordmark, condensed) sits on solid color panels or low-contrast image areas — never busy. Stylized people only at sunset or in motion, never staged direct-to-camera.

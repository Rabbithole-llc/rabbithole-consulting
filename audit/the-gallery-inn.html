With 4.2 stars across 645 reviews and a 16,900-follower Instagram, it's not because they're better, it's because Google can't read your site as a hotel and you're not ranking for the category.

If we worked together, here's the order

  1. Make Google read you. Add hotel structured data and wire the rating in so your 4.2 surfaces in hotel results and AI assistants.
  2. Get into the category. Combine the structured data with site speed and a local profile push so "boutique hotel San Juan" returns you in the top 10.
  3. Speed up mobile. Bring mobile performance up from 44 / 100 to match the desktop 80 score.
  4. Claim Booking.com. A claimed, price-matched listing on the largest demand source that feeds your Cloudbeds direct rate.

The competitive picture

Search "boutique hotel in San Juan" and the top results are Olive Boutique Hotel, SLH, and Acacia Boutique Hotel, Fortaleza isn't among them. The reviews are there; site speed and the fundamentals are what keep you out.

If we worked together, here's the order

  1. Rebuild for speed. A mobile-performance pass, image-weight first, so the page loads on a phone in under a couple of seconds.
  2. Open a direct booking path. A clear booking flow on your own site so guests stop defaulting to the OTAs.
  3. Close the security basics. Add the standard security headers across the site.
  4. Wire the rating in. Populate the rating field in the existing hotel tagging so Google can show your 4.2.

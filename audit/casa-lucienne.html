If we worked together, here's the order

  3. Get into the category. Structured data plus a fuller site plus a stronger local profile bring you into "boutique hotel San Juan" results.
  4. Close the small fixes. Move the Instagram link to https, and a focused mobile-performance pass to match the desktop 93.

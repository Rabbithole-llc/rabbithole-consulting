Beyond the audit: how we work with boutique hotels

Fixing what's above is the start. Beyond it, Rabbithole builds custom front-office infrastructure for your hotel, not a menu of plug-in products, but a coherent system that runs reservations, guest communication, and growth around your property.

Operations

AI Receptionist
A voice agent that answers every call to the property, books rooms over the phone, transfers genuine emergencies to staff, and follows up by SMS, so nights, weekends, and overflow stop costing you reservations.
Unified Guest Inbox
Phone, SMS, WhatsApp, Booking.com messages, email, and Instagram DMs land in one threaded inbox per guest, so a single person on shift can respond fast without juggling six tabs.
Direct Booking Chat
A booking-aware chat on your site that quotes live availability, answers room and policy questions, and pushes guests through to checkout, so the people who already trust your brand stop bouncing to an OTA.
AI Review Responses
Drafts personalized public replies to every new review on Google, TripAdvisor, Booking, and Expedia within hours, in your voice and approved before posting, so recent 1★ and 2★ stop dragging your rank.
Pre-Arrival Sequences
Automated, well-timed pre-arrival messages handle parking, check-in, upsells (early check-in, late checkout, upgrades), and local recommendations, so guests arrive oriented and revenue per stay lifts.
Rate Parity & Channel Profitability
Monitors your rates across every OTA in near-real-time, flags parity violations, and surfaces true margin per channel after commissions, so you know which platforms actually pay and which to deprioritize.
Daily Briefing & Live Calendar
A single morning briefing on arrivals, departures, special requests, and revenue pace, plus a live occupancy calendar, so the front desk and ownership see the property the same way every morning.

Marketing & growth

Brand Engine
Locks in your voice, photography style, and visual system so every channel - site, social, ads, email, booking flow - feels like one property, not a patchwork.
Content Generation
On-brand photo, copy, and short-video output at the cadence your channels need, drawn from real moments at the property rather than stock footage that flattens the place.
Content Calendar & Scheduling
A 60-day calendar of posts across Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and email, scheduled and published automatically with current rates and seasonal offers built into the messaging.
Paid Ads
Auto-paced campaigns across Google, Meta, TikTok, and LinkedIn that shift budget toward what's actually returning, with creative refreshed before fatigue rather than after.
Marketing Analytics & Attribution
Tracks every booking - direct, OTA, ad-driven, organic - back to the channel and touchpoint that produced it, so you stop guessing what marketing spend is actually doing.
AI Discovery (GEO / AEO)
Optimizes how ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini describe your property when a traveler asks them for the best {{CATEGORY_LOCAL}}, the new search front door.
Distinctiveness Audit
Quarterly comparison of your brand, copy, photography, and positioning against your direct competitors so you stop sounding like every other {{COMPETITIVE_SET}}.

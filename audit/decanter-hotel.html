Distribution & booking, the big picture

Direct booking is advertised on your homepage and you're on TripAdvisor and Yelp, but two of the big demand sources (Booking.com and Expedia) don't surface you. The bigger issue is upstream: Google can't read the site as a hotel, and the page gives Google no description to show, so the category search and the rich hotel results both miss you.
